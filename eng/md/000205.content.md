## inn (id: 280095)

An **inn** is a general term for a place where travelers can stay. This could be a place where people had to pay some money so that they could stay there, but often it meant a room in someone's house that was reserved for guests. When relatives or friends came to visit, they could stay in this room.

* **Associated Passages:** LUK 2:1–LUK 2:21; LUK 10:25–LUK 10:37
