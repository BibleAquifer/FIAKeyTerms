## adonai (id: 341027)

The word **Adonai** is a word in the Hebrew language, which is the language of the Old Testament. Adonai means lord, or master. The Jews often use this word as a respectful title to talk about God.

* **Associated Passages:** GEN 15:1–GEN 15:21; GEN 20:1–GEN 20:18
* **Associated Articles:** Jew (ID: `280105@FIAKeyTerms`); Jew (ID: `341109@FIAKeyTerms`)
