## hymn (id: 280156)

A **hymn** is a song of praise to God. Jewish people usually patterned them after the psalms. Jewish people sang praises of God when they met together in the synagogue. Jesus and his disciples sang a hymn during the last meal they had together. The followers of Jesus also sang hymns when they came together.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 14:12–MRK 14:26; MAT 26:26–MAT 26:35
