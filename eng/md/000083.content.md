## Decapolis (id: 280112)

**Decapolis** literally means "10 cities." The word refers to an area east of the Sea of Galilee and the Jordan river. Most people that lived there were not Jews.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 5:1–MRK 5:20; MRK 7:31–MRK 7:37
* **Associated Articles:** Jew (ID: `280105@FIAKeyTerms`); Jew (ID: `341109@FIAKeyTerms`); Jordan River (ID: `280170@FIAKeyTerms`); Sea of Galilee (ID: `280209@FIAKeyTerms`); Jordan River (ID: `341186@FIAKeyTerms`); Sea of Galilee (ID: `341231@FIAKeyTerms`)
