## heal (id: 280187)

In this kind of healing, Jesus **heals** the person both physically and spiritually, restoring him or her to community. The person has been made whole or complete.

* **Associated Passages:** LUK 17:11–LUK 17:19; MRK 5:21–MRK 5:34; MRK 6:45–MRK 6:56; MRK 8:22–MRK 8:26; MRK 10:46–MRK 10:52; MRK 1:40–MRK 1:45; ACT 28:1–ACT 28:10; LUK 18:35–LUK 19:10; LUK 10:1–LUK 10:16
