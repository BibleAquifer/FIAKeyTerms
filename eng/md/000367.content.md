## strength (id: 280188)

**Strength** refers to someone's ability to do something, or their physical strength. The words "heart, soul, mind, and strength" all function together to describe all of a person's personality and abilities.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 12:28–MRK 12:34
* **Associated Articles:** mind (ID: `280053@FIAKeyTerms`); mind (ID: `341049@FIAKeyTerms`); soul (ID: `280166@FIAKeyTerms`); heart (ID: `280173@FIAKeyTerms`); soul (ID: `341182@FIAKeyTerms`); heart (ID: `341189@FIAKeyTerms`); strength (ID: `280188@FIAKeyTerms`); strength (ID: `341207@FIAKeyTerms`)
