## son (id: 280081)

A **son** is the male child of his parents. But people can call someone "son" even if they are not related at all. If you call someone "son," this means you are speaking to them in a very friendly way. It is usually an older person who calls a younger person "son."

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 12:1–MRK 12:12; MRK 2:1–MRK 2:12; LUK 20:41–LUK 20:44
