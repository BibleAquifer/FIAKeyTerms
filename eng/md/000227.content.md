## justice (id: 341029)

Justice is when someone treats someone else fairly according to the law. When justice is happening in an entire society, then everybody treats each other equally and fairly. In a just society, people in authority punish those who do wrong and protect people who are poor and weak.

* **Associated Passages:** LUK 18:1–LUK 18:17; MAT 23:23–MAT 23:28; GEN 18:16–GEN 18:33; EXO 23:1–EXO 23:9
* **Associated Articles:** authority (ID: `280107@FIAKeyTerms`); authority (ID: `341112@FIAKeyTerms`)
