## king (id: 341020)

A king is someone who rules over a city or territory, like a region or a country. In the time of the Bible, a king normally ruled for life\-in other words, in other words, until he died. After his death, one of his sons would become king in his place.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 13:9–MRK 13:23; GEN 14:1–GEN 14:16; GEN 14:17–GEN 14:24; GEN 17:1–GEN 17:27; GEN 20:1–GEN 20:18; JHN 19:1–JHN 19:16; LUK 14:25–LUK 14:35; JHN 19:17–JHN 19:30; JHN 18:28–JHN 18:40
