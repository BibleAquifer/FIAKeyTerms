## Gerasenes (id: 280212)

These are different names for an area and cities within that area that is located along the southeast coast of the Sea of Galilee. Most people who lived here were not Jews. The area was part of the Decapolis.

* **Associated Passages:** MRK 5:1–MRK 5:20
* **Associated Articles:** Jew (ID: `280105@FIAKeyTerms`); Jew (ID: `341109@FIAKeyTerms`); Decapolis (ID: `280112@FIAKeyTerms`); Sea of Galilee (ID: `280209@FIAKeyTerms`); Decapolis (ID: `341118@FIAKeyTerms`); Sea of Galilee (ID: `341231@FIAKeyTerms`)
